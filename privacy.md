# WhatBot – Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** April 2026

## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains what data **WhatBot** ("the Bot") collects and how it is used.

By using the Bot, you agree to this policy.

---

## 2. Data Collected

WhatBot only collects data necessary for its functionality, including:

* **Discord User IDs**
* **Discord Server (Guild) IDs**
* **Channel IDs**
* **Auction data** (bids, winners)
* **Basket data** (items and totals)
* **Buyers list data** (for buyers-only giveaways)
* **Server configuration** (themes, payment links, auction channel)

No personal data such as real names, addresses, or payment details is collected by the Bot.

---

## 3. How Data Is Used

Collected data is used solely to:

* Operate auctions and track bids
* Manage baskets and totals
* Run giveaways and determine winners
* Store server-specific settings
* Ensure proper bot functionality

Data is **not used for advertising or profiling**.

The Bot may process data temporarily in memory during operation.

---

## 4. Data Storage

* Data is stored locally in JSON files or on the hosting environment
* Data may persist across bot restarts
* Data may be deleted at any time without notice

The developer takes reasonable steps to protect stored data but cannot guarantee absolute security.

---

## 5. Data Sharing

WhatBot does **not**:

* Sell data
* Share data with third parties
* Use external analytics or tracking services

All data remains internal to the Bot.

---

## 6. User Control

Server owners and moderators can:

* Clear basket data (`/clearallbasket`)
* Reset buyers (`/newstream`)
* Change server configuration at any time

If you want data removed, contact the bot owner.

---

## 7. Third-Party Services

WhatBot may link to external payment services such as:

* PayPal
* Ko-fi
* Shopify (future)

These services operate independently and have their own privacy policies.

WhatBot does **not** access or store payment information.

---

## 8. Data Retention

Data is retained only as long as necessary for bot functionality.

The developer may:

* Reset data
* Remove inactive data
* Wipe all data if the bot is discontinued

---

## 9. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated at any time.

Continued use of the Bot means you accept any changes.
